# Discover

Use this workflow when the user has no sufficiently concrete direction.

## Process

1. Inventory founder assets: domain access, trusted communities, distribution, skills, lived experience, capital, data, and partners.
2. Identify repeated, costly, urgent, or poorly served jobs within that access.
3. Generate three to five materially different directions. Do not produce cosmetic variations of one trend.
4. For each direction, specify customer, painful job, current workaround, why the founder can reach the customer, smallest test, and evidence still needed.
5. Compare directions on founder access, problem urgency, customer reachability, distribution advantage, buildability, and existing evidence.
6. Recommend one direction for testing, not for unconditional building.

## Required Output

- founder advantage and constraints
- three to five directions
- comparative reasoning with explicit unknowns
- recommended direction and confidence
- first validation experiment
- success threshold and kill or pivot condition
- what not to build yet

Do not use trend popularity as proof of customer demand. Do not invent market size or willingness to pay.
