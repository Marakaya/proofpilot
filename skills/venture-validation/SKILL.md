# Venture Validation

## Process

1. Restate the idea in one concrete sentence.
2. Identify the customer and painful job-to-be-done.
3. Map alternatives and comparable projects.
4. Score evidence quality.
5. Identify the riskiest assumptions.
6. Design the smallest validation test.

## Output

- idea summary
- customer hypothesis
- alternatives
- evidence map
- risk list
- validation plan
- go, pivot, or pause recommendation
