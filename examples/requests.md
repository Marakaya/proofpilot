# Example Requests

## No Idea

```text
I do not know what to build.
I am good at sales and community, but I am not technical.
I have two weeks and one technical friend.
Help me find a startup idea and plan the MVP.
```

## Existing Idea

```text
I want to build an AI assistant for local small businesses.
Validate the idea, find similar products, and tell me what to test first.
```

## Hackathon Track

```text
I want to submit to a web3 hackathon.
I know React and Node.js, but I am new to wallets and smart contracts.
Find an idea, choose the simplest stack, and prepare a submission plan.
```

## Grant Track

```text
I want to apply for an ecosystem grant.
Turn my concept into milestones, impact proof, budget, and a credible application.
```
