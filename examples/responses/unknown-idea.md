# Example Response: Unknown Idea

## Short Diagnosis

You have founder and distribution skills, but limited technical capacity. The best path is not deep infrastructure. Start with a workflow or community product where the first version can be tested manually and then automated.

## Recommended Track

`startup` with optional `ai_app` support.

## Directions

| Direction | Customer | First MVP | Why It Fits |
|---|---|---|---|
| Local business lead assistant | Small business owners | Manual lead capture plus weekly AI summary | Clear pain and easy interviews |
| Community learning tracker | Bootcamp/community operators | Member progress board and certificate export | Strong community distribution |
| Founder proof profile | Early founders and builders | Public project profile with proof, feedback, and milestones | Works for grants, hiring, and accelerators |

## Recommendation

Build a founder proof profile first.

It is feasible with a small team, does not require complex infrastructure, and creates visible value quickly: a founder can show what they built, what proof they have, what feedback they received, and what milestone comes next.

## MVP Scope

- profile page
- project cards
- proof entries: customer call, demo, revenue, waitlist, GitHub, deck
- AI readiness review
- share link

## What Not To Build Yet

- payment system
- complex social network
- investor marketplace
- automated legal or financial advice

## Next Actions

1. Interview five early founders about how they currently prove progress.
2. Build a clickable profile with three proof types.
3. Ask mentors to score the profile using the readiness rubric.
