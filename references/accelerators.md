# Accelerator Readiness

Use this reference when the user asks about YC, Techstars, 500 Global, Antler, Entrepreneur First, Sequoia Arc, non-web3 accelerators, startup applications, accelerator readiness, or interview preparation.

Always apply `references/honest-evaluation.md` first.

## Data Files

- `data/accelerator-programs.json`: target program profiles, terms, fit, red flags, official links.
- `data/accelerator-rubrics.json`: accelerator readiness scorecard.
- `data/judgment-policy.json`: non-negotiable honesty standard.
- `data/source-playbooks.json`: source order and credential policy.

## Program Selection

Do not default every startup to YC.

Match the target:

| Program | Best Fit |
|---|---|
| YC | Clear startup with strong founders, fast progress, user pull, large market, and direct application answers. |
| Techstars | Startup that benefits from mentorship, vertical/geographic program fit, network, and post-program fundraising prep. |
| 500 Global | Functional product with early revenue/growth and a global scaling path. |
| Antler | Exceptional founder profile, early idea or cofounder-search stage, high ambition. |
| Entrepreneur First | Exceptional individual/founder talent, cofounder search, deep edge, or very early company formation. |
| Sequoia Arc | Pre-seed/seed company with elite ambition, customer insight, category narrative, and team-building potential. |

## YC-Style Evaluation

Use these questions:

1. What is the company making, in one matter-of-fact sentence?
2. Who desperately wants it?
3. What are they doing today instead?
4. What proof exists that people want it?
5. Why is this team unusually likely to win?
6. What has changed in the last 7 or 30 days?
7. Why can this become very large?
8. What is the strongest reason to reject this application?

If the answer to question 8 is decisive, lead with it.

## Application Review

For every answer:

- cut marketing-speak
- replace adjectives with facts
- replace market claims with wedge and customer proof
- make founder edge concrete
- state traction honestly
- say "not enough evidence" when traction is weak

## Readiness Verdict

Use one of:

- `apply_now`
- `apply_after_7_day_sprint`
- `wait`
- `pivot`

Do not recommend `apply_now` unless the application has a specific product, credible founder edge, and at least one meaningful proof point.

## 7-Day Sprint

If the idea is close but weak, prescribe a 7-day sprint:

- 10 to 20 customer conversations with a narrow persona
- one demo or concierge workflow
- one measurable traction target
- one competitor teardown
- rewrite the application after the sprint

## Output Shape

Return:
